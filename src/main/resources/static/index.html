Simply Search

Say goodbye to the time-consuming process of combing through countless job listings. GrowHire quickly scans LinkedIn for opportunities tailored to your profile and uses your resume along with ChatGPT’s insights to pinpoint the perfect roles for you. 💼✨

The Results

Each job listing is displayed in a clear table. 📊
ChatGPT assigns a score to every job! ⭐
Higher scores indicate a better match for you. 👍

Best of all, these results were delivered in under
5 minutes 🚀 so you can focus on what matters most. ⏱️

Feed Scroller

No more endless scrolling! GrowHire automatically spots and saves the posts that truly matter by matching them against your custom Keywords.txt file. If a post features your chosen keywords, it’s instantly saved—ensuring you always have the important updates at your fingertips. 💡💾

Expend Your network

Expand your network effortlessly! GrowHire scans the people section on LinkedIn and automatically sends connection requests, streamlining your outreach and saving you time. 🤝🚀
